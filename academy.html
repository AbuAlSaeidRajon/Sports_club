Welcome to কোমরপুর বিজয় নিশান ক্লাব Cricket Academy

Our cricket academy is dedicated to nurturing and developing young cricket talent. We provide top-notch training programs, experienced coaches, and excellent facilities to help aspiring cricketers achieve their dreams.

Training Programs

Junior Program

Designed for young cricketers aged 7-12, this program focuses on developing basic cricket skills, understanding the game, and fostering a love for cricket.

Intermediate Program

For cricketers aged 13-17, this program aims to enhance technical skills, physical fitness, and tactical understanding of the game.

Advanced Program

Our advanced program is tailored for cricketers aged 18 and above who aspire to play at competitive levels. It includes intensive training, match simulations, and personalized coaching.

Our Facilities

We boast state-of-the-art facilities to ensure the best training environment for our players:

  • High-quality cricket pitches
  • Indoor practice nets
  • Modern gym and fitness center
  • Video analysis room
  • Comfortable player lounge
