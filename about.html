About কোমরপুর বিজয় নিশান ক্লাব

Welcome to কোমরপুর বিজয় নিশান ক্লাব, a premier sports club dedicated to nurturing and promoting sports talent in our community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for young athletes to develop their skills and participate in various sports activities. We aim to foster a love for sports, teamwork, and a healthy lifestyle among our members.

Our History

Founded in 1990, কোমরপুর বিজয় নিশান ক্লাব has a rich history of achievements and contributions to the local sports community. Over the years, we have produced several top athletes who have represented us at national and international levels.

1990

Club founded with a vision to promote sports in the local community.

2000

First major tournament win, establishing our presence in the region.

2010

Expanded our facilities to include a state-of-the-art training ground.

2020

Celebrated our 30th anniversary with a series of community events and tournaments.

Our Values

At কোমরপুর বিজয় নিশান ক্লাব, we believe in the values of integrity, teamwork, excellence, and community. These values guide us in all our endeavors and help us create a positive impact on our members and the community.
